HOW TO GET THERE

To travel from the Philippines to Japan, you'll need a valid passport and a tourist visa, unless you're from a visa-exempt country. Depending on current regulations, a negative COVID-19 test or vaccination record may also be required. Once in Japan, you can reach Shibuya Sky by taking the JR Yamanote Line to Shibuya Station and then walking to the Shibuya Scramble Square building. For Universal Studios Japan, take the JR Osaka Loop Line to Universal City Station. To visit Fushimi Inari Taisha, take the JR Nara Line to Inari Station. If you're visiting the shrine, don’t forget to wear comfortable shoes, as you'll likely walk through the iconic torii gates.
